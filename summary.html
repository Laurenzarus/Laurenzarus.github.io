Final Project Summary

Topic/Purpose: My final project is simply a sort of personal website that describes me: a kind of online resume, essentially. I wanted to create a website that can tell viewers information about me while also being mildly entertaining (AKA not as boring as reading the info off a piece of paper).

Special Features: I have imported two different Google Fonts into my website, which I use on every single page. I am also using two different jQuery plugins on separate pages. The names of the plugins, along with links to their documentation, are below:

  1. JQuery Timespace: https://www.jqueryscript.net/time-clock/Timeline-Slider-jQuery-Timespace.html
  2. JQuery skdslider: https://www.jqueryscript.net/slider/Responsive-Full-Width-Slider-Plugin-For-jQuery-skdslider.html
